Answers

Frequently asked questions

Who is Structured in 6 for?
It's designed for anyone who feels stuck, overwhelmed or inconsistent with their health and fitness. If you've struggled to build lasting habits, this programme helps you regain control and create a routine that actually sticks.
Is this a weight loss programme?
Weight loss may happen, but the primary goal is to build the habits, routines and mindset that make long-term results possible. Think of it as laying the foundations for lasting success.
Will I receive a personalised nutrition plan?
Yes. You'll receive personalised nutrition guidance, but the focus is on creating a sustainable way of eating rather than chasing perfection.
Will I receive a workout programme?
Yes. Your training programme will be tailored to your experience, goals and available equipment.
How often will I check in?
You'll complete a weekly check-in with Mieke, who will review your progress, provide feedback and make any necessary adjustments.
Are the live coaching calls mandatory?
No. They're there to provide extra education, accountability and support, but recordings will be available if you can't attend.
What if I miss a workout or go off track?
That's exactly what this programme is for. We'll help you get back on track without guilt, so one setback doesn't become a bad week or month.
Can I join if I'm a complete beginner?
Absolutely. The programme is designed to meet you where you are and gradually build confidence and consistency.
Should I do Structured in 6 before Transformed in 12?
If you're struggling with consistency, routine or sticking to a plan, yes. Structured in 6 is designed to prepare you so you get even better results from Transformed in 12.
Who is Transformed in 12 for?
This programme is for people who are ready to fully commit to transforming their body. If you've already built some consistency and want to maximise your results, this is for you.
What's different about this compared to Structured in 6?
Transformed in 12 focuses on optimising every aspect of your progress. Nutrition, training and activity targets are more specific, with the goal of achieving the best body composition results possible.
Will my plan be personalised?
Yes. Your calories, nutrition, training and progression are tailored specifically to you and adjusted throughout the programme.
Who will be coaching me?
Emily will personally coach you throughout the programme and review your weekly check-ins.
Do I have to attend the live coaching calls?
No. They are available as an additional resource, but attendance is optional.
Can beginners join?
Yes, although if you're struggling to build consistent habits or maintain a routine, Structured in 6 may be the better place to start.
Will I lose weight?
The goal is body transformation. Depending on your goals, that may mean fat loss, building muscle, improving body composition or increasing strength.
What happens after the 12 weeks?
Many clients continue their journey into ongoing coaching, while others maintain their results independently using everything they've learned.
